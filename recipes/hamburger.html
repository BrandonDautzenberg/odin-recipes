Hamburger

"Be sure to eat the burger with the grease dribbling down your chin" ~ George R.R. Martin

Description

The hamburger is one of the most well-known dishes in the world. It is typically sold at fast-food restaurants. It is a highly versatile dish and can be made as mundane or exotic as you would like. For this recipe however, we'll stick to the classic meat and cheese hamburger

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • cheese (preferably cheddar)
  • sesame seed buns
  • ketchup or mayonaise
  • lettuce

Steps

  1. Dump the ground beef in a bowl, add salt and pepper.
  2. Shape the burgers into patties. Make them nice and big as they will shrink once cooked.
  3. Sear the patties in a pan, and flip them sometimes.
  4. Toast your buns and add the cheese, lettuce and your condiment of choice
